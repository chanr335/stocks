Commands:

- stock (symbol..., datetime) : symbol(s) at a point in time
- etf (symbol..., datetime) : etf symbol(s) at a point in time
- index (symbol..., datetime) : index symbol(s) at a point in time
- exchange (type) : list out exchanges
- timeseries (symbol, timerange)
- exchangerate (currency1, currency2)
- marketstate (ticker) : check symbol's market state
- quote (ticker): see company quotes
- profile (ticker) : TBD

Features:

- graph visuals
- price table visuals
- calendar date picker
